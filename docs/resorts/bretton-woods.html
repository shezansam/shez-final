A short guide to spending your winter vacations on the slopes

Bretton Woods

Location: Bretton Woods, New Hampshire

Closest Airport: Manchester-Boston Regional Airport (MHT)

Elevation: 3,100 feet / 945 meters

Ticket Price: Adult lift tickets range from $82 to $109 USD for a one-day ticket

Located in the White Mountains, Bretton Woods has alpine skiing, nordic skiing, snowshoeing, and tubing. The ski resort has 10 lifts and 98 trails. 29% of runs are beginner, 39% are intermediate, and 42% are advanced.
