Telluride

Location: Telluride, Colorado

Closest Airport: Montrose Regional Airport (MTJ)

Elevation: 13,150 feet / 4,008 meters

Ticket Price: Adult lift tickets are $215 USD for a one-day ticket

Located in the San Juan Mountains, Telluride is a popular resort that provides a 360-degree view of the surrounding mountains. The ski resort has 148 trails and 19 lifts. 23% of trails are beginner, 36% are intermediate, and 41% are advanced.
