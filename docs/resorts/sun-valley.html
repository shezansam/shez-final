Sun Valley

  • Location: Sun Valley, Idaho

  • Closest Airport: Friedman Memorial Airport (SUN)

  • Elevation: 9,150 feet / 2,789 meters

  • Ticket Price: Adult lift tickets range from $99 to $125 USD for a one-day ticket and $98 for a half-day ticket.

Located in the Smoky Mountains, Sun Valley features two mountains- Bald Mountain and Dollar Mountain. The ski resort has 17 lifts and 120 trails. 36% of runs are beginner, 42% are intermediate, and 22% are advanced.
