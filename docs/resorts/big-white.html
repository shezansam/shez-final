Big White

Location: Kelowna, British Columbia

Closest Airport: Kelowna International Airport (YLW)

Elevation: 11,212 feet / 3,418 meters

Ticket Price: Adult lift tickets range from $109 to $189 CAD for a one-day ticket

Located in the Beaverdell Range, Big White has some of the cleanest and freshest tap water in the world. The ski resort has 16 lifts and 119 trails. 18% of runs are beginner, 54% are intermediate, and 28% are advanced.
