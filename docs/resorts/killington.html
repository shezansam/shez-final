Travel Guide to North American Ski Resorts

A short guide to spending your winter vacations on the slopes

Killington

  • Location: Killington, Vermont

  • Closest Airport: Burlington International Airport (BTV)

  • Elevation: 4,241 feet / 1,293 meters

  • Ticket Price: Adult lift tickets are range from $64 to $182 USD for a one-day ticket

Located in the Green Mountains, Killington hosts the Womens Audi FIS Ski World Cup every year. The ski resort has 155 trails and 21 lifts. 28% of trails are beginner, 33% are intermediate, and 39% are advanced.
