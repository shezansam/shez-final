Top Ski Resorts in the United States

Below you will find the top ski resorts in the United States. Located in a variety of states across the U.S., these beautiful resorts will give you access to cozy towns and breathtaking views.

Aspen Snowmass
Snowmass Village, Colorado

With a top elevation of 12,510 feet or 3,813 meters, Aspen Snowmass has 98 runs. Fly in Denver International Airport (DEN) and it is a 3 hr 48 min drive to the ski resort.
